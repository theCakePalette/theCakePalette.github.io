OUR STORY

The Cake Palette is the brain-child of Zainab Rampurawala who considers herself lucky to have taken the risk and move from the hustle and bustle of a big city and corporate life to a small hill station surrounded by green valleys and succeeded in making her dreams come true (read: yummy).
Started in 2016, The Cake Palette operates out of a small studio celebrating home baking traditions, specialising in wedding cakes, custom cakes, cupcakes, desserts and treats made from scratch using only high quality ingredients. Our delicious cookies, cupcakes, tarts and brownies make for a perfect party return gift for any occasion such as birthdays, weddings, anniversary, engagement, baby showers, corporate events to name a few.

We are an appointment only business which enables us to create cakes that are personal, unique and individually tailored for each client.
We also provide delivery in and around Lonavala.
