Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Lonavala?
We deliver cookies and brownies at a location in Pune (no delivery charges), the address of which can be found in the CONTACT US page.
For wedding/custom cakes, delivery at the event location in Pune is available at an additional cost of 1000 INR.
For bulk orders of cupcakes, cake pops and tarts exceeding 8000 INR, delivery at the event location in Pune is available at additional cost of 1000 INR.
Do your cakes contain egg? Do you make eggless versions?
Yes, most of our cakes contain egg as it is the single most versatile baking ingredient. It provides structure, stability, texture, flavour and moisture to the cakes. Swiss and Italian meringue buttercream also contain eggs giving a silky, smooth and perfectly sweet buttercream.
If you need an eggless cake, all the classic cakes can be made eggless. However, all our Gourmet Cakes will contain egg.
Cupcakes, Cookies, Cake Pops, Brownies and Tarts can be made eggless if specified.
Cream Puffs contain eggs.
What is the lead-time to make wedding/custom cake order?
At least 1 month, we take orders 6 months in advance. For an exquisitely designed cake, ordering between 1-2 months in advance is the best; loads of time to discuss about details, book the slot (as full reservation only occurs when the 50% deposit is received) and to create your cake. Deal is, the earlier the more time we have to discuss and design your specialty cake.
Do you do fondant cakes?
Yes, we do fondant cakes but not during rainy season due to high humidity levels in Lonavala which makes the fondant absorb moisture and become sticky. Also we don't model a human or animal figure with fondant for cake toppers. In case your theme needs to, please speak to us to find out how we can incorporate your theme in different way. Also we spesialize in buttercream texture cakes. Visit our BLOG section to view beautiful buttercream cakes.
Can I change the design of custom cake after I have set it?
Once the design is set during appointment, reasonable changes to your order may be made; additional charges may be incurred depending on the nature of the changes.
However no changes can be made 2 weeks prior to the event date.
Is a deposit required to secure my cake order? What methods of payment do you accept?
Yes. A 50% deposit is required to secure the slot. As all our cake orders are taken via appointments, the earlier you reach us, the higher the chances you will be able to secure the slot.
For all cake orders, you may do so through Internet Banking Get in touch to know the account details.
Paytm +91-8380090875
Does the price include delivery and set up? Can I do self collection?
No. The price we quote you is purely for the cake design. Delivery and set up is charged separately between 200 INR to 1000 INR depending on the size of cake and your event location.
We welcome self-collection but you will have to take care of your order. Our responsibility ends once the cake is out of our studio. For tall and heavy cakes, we would strongly recommend you to have us deliver it for you.
Can you do a cake design exactly how we referred you to?
We strongly believe that design and creation of the cake is an art and is subjected to minor deviations from the design or description. Photos of cakes by other designers will only be used as inpiration.
