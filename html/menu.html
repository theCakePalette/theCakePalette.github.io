Custom Party Cakes
Cakes have forever been a staple centre piece of all occasions. To ensure everything is picture perfect on your big day, we invite you to call us for a one-on-one consultation. Because all our cakes are one-of-a-kind creations, final pricing depends on the size and design. We also offer delivery and set-up at the venue. Every cake is meticulously designed for long hours and uniquely tailored for our clients therefore a minimum of a fortnight is required for the complete execution of tier cakes. Since we take limited orders, it is recommended to book your dates as early as you can. Refer our Cakes Menu for flavour options.
